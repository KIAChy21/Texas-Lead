About This Map

This map shows the distribution of Lead contamination and its health impact to Texas's under 6 years of children. Project Overview: This project aims to map lead exposure risks in Texas by integrating industrial pollution, socioeconomic data, and child health vulnerability indicators. Using GIS and data science techniques, I identify high-risk zones and provide actionable insights. Research Question: How do geolocation, socioeconomic conditions, and industrial toxic discharges contribute to increased lead exposure risks among children under five in Texas?
